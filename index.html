About

I come from India. My journey into engineering began at home, breaking open RC cars and game consoles out of curiosity. That same curiosity led me to computers, and eventually to systems programming.

Influenced by the greats like Alan Turing and John von Neumann, and guided by mentors such as Prof. Jyotiprakash Mishra, I am drunk and stoned deep into the nasha of embedded systems, systems-level programming, and VLSI engineering. I believe that understanding the core of how things work; from screws to semiconductors; enables real innovation.
